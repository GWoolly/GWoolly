About

Hello and welcome!

I’m Grant, a video game developer from the historic naval city of Portsmouth.

I help developers build high-quality, engaging game experiences by ensuring smooth, bug-free gameplay, streamlining testing with custom automation tools, and exploring innovative design solutions.

With almost a decade in the games industry, I’ve ventured around the UK, collaborating with some of the most knowledgeable creative teams at Aardman, Jagex, Rare, and Dlala Studios to name a few! These experiences have not only shaped my technical skills but also sparked my passion for delving deeper into game design.

When I’m not vanquishing bugs, refining game mechanics, or serving cheesy jokes, you can find me experimenting with level design on my personal projects, adventuring in the countryside, or cultivating chillies and other fresh veggies.

I currently serve as the Lead QA Tester at Aardman Animations on Chicken Run 2: Eggstraction, a dynamic project in partnership with Netflix.
